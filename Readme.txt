
Written by Gary Quinn, 14th April 2013,  Quinn.Gary@ntlworld.com

To produce summary files from the raw log files of the Dylos Air Quality Monitor.

Note:  The Dylos DC1100 sometimes sends 61 values in an hour and sometimes send 60.

Standard disclaimer:  Use/Edit/Modify at your own risk.  This software is under development. It is likely to contain bugs and undocumented features.

Other standard stuff:  Let me know if you find any bugs, or make any changes/corrections.
